Operating Principles

01

Own the Outcome

Be the directly-responsible individual. When production needs you, jump in — whether that means debugging at 2 AM or making a deployment call under pressure.

02

Close the Gaps

Continuously identify what's missing — in tooling, observability, automation, validation, process — and build plans to fix it. Don't wait for perfect.

03

Ship Reliably at Scale

Production deployment must be reliable, repeatable, and safe. Define readiness criteria, validate rigorously, and deploy with confidence.

04

Lead Through Ambiguity

When the hardware, tooling, and operating model are still being built, drive clarity through strong judgment, cross-functional alignment, and bias for action.
